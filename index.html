Problem Statement

Many daily-paying customers miss or delay repayments, increasing loan default risk and operational costs.

Solution

WSRA automates repayment monitoring, assigns risk levels, and sends SMS alerts to improve repayment behavior.

Workflow

Customer input → Risk analysis → SMS generation → Alert sending → History tracking

Risk Assessment

Low Risk: 0 missed payments
Medium Risk: 1 missed payment
High Risk: 2+ missed payments

SMS Alerts

Automated messages are sent based on customer repayment behavior.

Experiment Plan

Pilot WSRA for 4–6 weeks and measure repayment improvement and customer engagement.
